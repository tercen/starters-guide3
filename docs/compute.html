Chapter 9 Compute

In this section you perform a computation. The projection you created in the previous section can also be used by a computation (i.e. operator). This is due to the What you see is what you compute paradigm. The following steps outlines how to add an operator.

  • Click on the Operator tab

  • Go to the Public tab and select the PCA operator, click OK

  • Click on the save icon of the Data step page bar The save icon (see red circle in figure below) will disappear once it is saved

  • Go to the workflow page

  • Right click on the data step and select Run The the data step status color will now go from blue to red (i.e in progress). Wait until the status goes to green (i.e completed).
